BRIYANI

Spiced rice with meat (chicken, beef, or mutton).
  1. Chicken Biryani

    2. Made with marinated chicken, fragrant basmati rice, and a blend of aromatic spices, chicken biryani is one of the most popular and widely enjoyed variations.

  2. Beef Biryani

    3. This hearty version features tender beef chunks cooked with flavorful spices and layered with rice, creating a rich and robust dish loved across Pakistan.

  3. Mutton Biryani

    4. Prepared with succulent goat meat, mutton biryani is known for its deep flavor and aromatic spices, making it a favorite for festive occasions and special gatherings.

  4. Sada Aloo Biryani

    5. A vegetarian-friendly option, aloo biryani features spiced potatoes cooked with aromatic rice and traditional biryani masala, offering a delicious and simple alternative to meat-based biryanis.

PULAO

Mildly flavored rice cooked with broth, meat, or vegetables.
